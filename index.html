Our Story

A Heritage of Flavour

At SpiceTick, we believe every great dish begins with the finest spices. Rooted in the rich spice-growing traditions of Kerala's Western Ghats, we bring you nature's most exquisite flavours carefully handpicked, sun-dried, and packed to preserve their essence.

Our three signature spices — Cardamom, the Queen of Spices; Cloves, the aromatic powerhouse; and Pepper, the King of Spices — are sourced from trusted farming families in Idukki, ensuring purity, quality, and sustainability in every batch.
